Multiline

Multiline with tag and label

\[ \begin{eqnarray} \sin^2(x)+\cos^2(x) &=& 1, \qquad \forall x\in\mathbb R \tag{A} \label{eq:A} \end{eqnarray} \]

which can then be referred by \(\eqref{eq:A}\), easy enough.

Array of equations:

\[ \begin{eqnarray} \sin^2(x)+\cos^2(x) - 1 &=& 0\nonumber\\ |\exp(i\theta)|-1 &=& 0. \end{eqnarray} \]
